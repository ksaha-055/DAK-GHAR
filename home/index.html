What is Dak Ghar Niryat Kendra

Dhak Ghar Niryat Kendra is a postal export center (PEN) operated by the Indian Postal Department. It is a one-stop shop for businesses that want to export their products to foreign countries. PENs offer a variety of services, including:

  • Comprehensive export documentation and processing: PENs help businesses with the necessary export documentation, such as shipping labels, customs forms, and certificates of origin.
  • Consolidated shipping: PENs can consolidate multiple shipments from different businesses into a single shipment, which can save businesses money.
  • International tracking: PENs provide businesses with international tracking information so they can track the progress of their shipments.
