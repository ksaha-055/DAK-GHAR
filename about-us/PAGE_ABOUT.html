ABOUT US

INTRODUCTION

Welcome to the official web portal of DNK, which stands for "Dak Ghar Niryat Kendra" or "Post Office Export Centre".It is a smart idea from India's Department of Posts (DoP) and the Central Board of Indirect Taxes and Customs (CBIC). We have been serving our nation with dedication and commitment for over 200 years. The Department of Posts is a vital part of the Indian government, and our mission is to help small businesses export their products hassle-free.

WHAT DNK DOES

DNK centers provide a simple and affordable way for small businesses to export. They use the post office network, which is already in place all over India, making it easy for small exporters to reach global customers. DNK is like a helping hand for small businesses, making it less complicated and more affordable to send their products worldwide.
